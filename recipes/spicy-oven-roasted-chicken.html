Ingredients

Below are the ingredients required to make this dish for 4 people.

  • Large Whole chicken
  • Spicy curry powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • 1 large tablespoon of natural yoghurt
  • 1 lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red dried chilli flakes

How to make a simple spicy spicy oven roasted chicken step by step

Simple steps to make the best spicy oven roasted chicken:

  1. Get a bowl to prepare marinade for chicken
  2. Add salt, pepper, 1 tbs of paprika, 2 tbs of curry powder, 2 tbs of coriander powder, 1 tbs of garlic powder and 1 large tbs of natural yoghurt
  3. Mixed well until the marinate is like a paste
  4. score the chicken on the breast and thighs
  5. Add marinade to chicken and rub well until you get good coverage
  6. Squeeze whole lemon all over the chicken
  7. Sprinkle chilli flakes across chicken - distribute well
  8. Put in fridge and allow to set for at least 18 hours
  9. Once chicken has been marinated. Take it out of the fridge
  10. Preheat oven to 200c
  11. Ensure chicken is in tray suitable for oven and cover the top with foil
  12. Leave chicken in oven to cook for at least 1 hour
  13. Take it out the oven and your ready to serve with your favourite sides

Now enjoy your chicken!
